Who I AM

Your Instant Midwife

Tasmin

I bring over 18 years of midwifery experience and a deep belief that every woman deserves to feel heard, supported, and confident throughout her pregnancy journey.

I qualified as a midwife in 2011 and have since built a practice rooted in holistic, empowering care. Trained in hypnobirthing, hypnotherapy, Reiki, and aromatherapy in pregnancy, I bring a gentle, whole-person approach to everything I do. As a mum of four and grandmother of two, I know firsthand that the journey into motherhood isn't always straightforward. Having navigated postpartum depression myself, returned to study as a single parent to four children, and balanced the demands of home and work life with determination, I bring a depth of personal understanding that goes far beyond clinical training. That lived experience is woven into everything I do.

When I'm not working, you'll find me on the dancefloor — salsa is my creative outlet and my freedom, a passion that reflects the same joy and expressiveness I bring to my practice. My love of research, languages, and travel means I'm always learning, always curious, and always bringing something new to the women I support.

I created My Instant Midwife to make expert midwifery support accessible to every woman, wherever she is and whenever she needs it.

MIM is an acronym of My Instant Midwife — but it means a little more than that. My grandson Oscar called me Mimmy when he was small, and as he grew, it became simply MIM. A name born from family, worn with pride, and now at the heart of everything I do.

I can't wait to walk alongside you.

OUR VALUES

At MIM, everything we do is guided by a set of values that put you at the centre of your care. These aren't just words — they are the principles that shape every interaction, every conversation, and every piece of guidance we provide. When you choose MIM, you choose a midwife who shows up for you with honesty, expertise, and heart.

ADVOCACY

Every woman deserves a voice in her own care. I believe in standing alongside you, helping you understand your options, ask the right questions, and feel confident in the decisions you make. You should never feel unheard or overlooked and with MIM, you won't be.

EMPOWERMENT

Knowledge is one of the most powerful things I can give you. My goal is to make sure you feel informed, prepared, and in control at every stage of your pregnancy and beyond. An empowered woman makes better decisions for herself and her baby and I'm here to help you get there.

COMPASSION

Pregnancy is one of the most significant journeys of your life, and it doesn't always go smoothly. Whether you're anxious, overwhelmed, or simply need someone to talk to, I bring genuine warmth and understanding to every interaction. You are never just a client — you are a person, and you matter.

EVIDENCE-BASED

The guidance I provide is always rooted in current, peer-reviewed research and best midwifery practice. You can trust that the advice you receive from MIM is not opinion or trend — it is grounded in evidence, delivered with honesty, and tailored to your individual needs.
